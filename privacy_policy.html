Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 6, 2026

Introduction

Nook's Music App ("the App") is developed by Einfach nur Baum. This privacy policy explains how the App handles your data.

Data Collection

Nook's Music App does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data or usage data.

The App operates entirely offline and locally on your device. It does not connect to any servers, APIs, or third-party services.

Permissions

The App requests the following permissions, all of which are used solely for local functionality:

  • Foreground Service (Media Playback) – Required to continue playing music when the App is in the background.
  • Notifications – Used to display the currently playing track in the notification bar.

No data from these permissions is collected, transmitted, or shared.

Audio Files

The App allows you to select audio files from your device's local storage. These files are accessed and played locally. They are never uploaded, copied, or shared with any third party.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party SDKs, analytics tools, advertising frameworks, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

The App does not collect any data from anyone, including children. It is safe for use by all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is updated, the changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
